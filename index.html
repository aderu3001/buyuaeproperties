Why Canadians are Choosing Dubai.

Beyond the skyline, Dubai offers a stable, investor-friendly environment that complements Canadian portfolios with high rental yields and tax efficiency.

01

Tax Efficiency

Enjoy 0% personal income tax on rental returns and no capital gains tax in the UAE. *Consult your Canadian tax advisor for local obligations.

02

High Rental Demand

With a growing expat population, Dubai consistently delivers net rental yields between 6-9%, significantly higher than major Canadian hubs.

03

Global Connectivity

A world-class infrastructure and a strategic location between East and West make it a resilient hub for global business and tourism.

04

Investor-Friendly Market

Transparent regulations, Golden Visa opportunities for property owners, and a highly regulated real estate authority (RERA).

05

Currency Stability

The UAE Dirham is pegged to the USD, providing a stable hedge against currency fluctuations for international investors.

06

Modern Infrastructure

From the world's tallest building to futuristic transport, Dubai's commitment to growth ensures long-term property appreciation.

Frequently Asked Questions.

Can I buy remotely from Canada?

Yes, 100%. We handle everything from virtual tours to digital signatures and remote booking. You don't need to be physically present in Dubai to secure your property.

What are the typical payment plans?

Most developers offer interest-free payment plans, often structured as 60/40 or 70/30, where a portion is paid during construction and the remainder at handover.

What areas are best for rental demand?

Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, and Business Bay are top for short-term rentals. For long-term family demand, areas like Dubai Hills Estate and JVC are highly recommended.

How does the buying process work?

It starts with a booking fee (usually 5-10%), followed by the signing of the Sales and Purchase Agreement (SPA) and registration with the Dubai Land Department.

What fees should I expect?

Standard fees include a 4% Dubai Land Department (DLD) fee and a small administrative fee. Our consultancy services for buyers are typically free as we are compensated by developers.

Can you help with property management?

Yes, we partner with leading management firms to ensure your property is rented out and maintained, providing you with a truly passive investment.

Is financing available to foreigners?

Yes, non-residents can typically get mortgages for up to 50% of the property value from UAE banks, subject to eligibility.

How fast can I get options?

Once you submit your requirements, we typically provide a curated shortlist within 24 hours.
